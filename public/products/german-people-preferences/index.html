This is a great poster all about German people and preferences. Excellent for learning loads of German vocabulary.

No matter where you travel in Germany there is one topic of conversation that is always popular – other people!

The ten characters on this poster cover a wide variety of ages and contemporary German roles; doctor, baby, boy, manager, barber, young woman, accountant, grandmother, waitress and a lawyer.

Through them you can learn how to use German vocabulary and phrases for colors, clothes, hair styles, emotions, preferences and characteristics – things that interest people and make for really good topics of conversation.
